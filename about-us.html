Hello, Welcome to Bake 'n Love!


At Bake 'n Love, we sell more than cakes. Our personal in-bakery experience, time-honored recipes and comforting taste have enhanced celebrations and special occasions throughout Bacoor and Kawit since 2020 during pandemic...and now Bake 'n Love is available nationwide.

Our History


Bake 'n Love founders, Maan Lim and Pia Andres, grew up in Cavite, where they were called bestfriends by their three beloved kids. Every day, after work, they would come home from office to bring cupcakes for their kids and enjoy it over a glass of milk. This daily baking ritual and special time together in the kitchen left a lasting impression. During pandemic, they have decided to build a small business to somehow earn while facing the challenges of COVID situation. They have shown their recipes to their fatherwho also love to bake cakes. The pure and simple connection those recipes fostered helped Maan and Pia to create Bake 'n Love online business and to share her experience with others. Through Bake 'n Love, Douglas, honors Maan and Pia: both in their dedication to baking, and his commitment to providing them with progressive careers in helping to grow the businesss.
